About Us

Established in 2010, Classic Cuts has been serving the community with top-quality barber services. Our experienced barbers are trained in both classic and modern techniques to give you the perfect look.

We specialize in men's haircuts, beard trims, hot towel shaves, and styling services. Our shop maintains the tradition of old-school barbering while keeping up with contemporary styles.

At Classic Cuts, we believe a great haircut is more than just a service - it's an experience. Our comfortable, masculine environment is designed to make you feel at home while we take care of your grooming needs.
